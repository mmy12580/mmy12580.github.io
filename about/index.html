About Me

I am now working as Principal Scientist for Course5i AI Lab, Toronto. My main focus is to build scalable and transferable B2B AI products and services to more than 20 countries and regions, e.g., Microsoft, Lenovo, Colgate Palmolive, and so on.

My main interest is to build an “end-to-end” human-computer interaction system given kinds of “knowledge”, including transfer learning, MTL, meta-learning, and self-supervised learning etc. If you are interested in my experience or the website, have a look at my resume for more information.

Why the blog?

I created this blog not only to help me refresh my memory about engineering details, theory behind models, training tricks, and optimization techniques, but also to communicate with people who read and share what they know or think, so we can expand our own “knowledge” base togeter.
