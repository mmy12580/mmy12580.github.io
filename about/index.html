About

I am a Machine Learning Scientist at WGames, currently focused on recommendation system, autoML, and natural language processing (NLP). In the meantime, I am also a NLP Scientist at Leafy.ai working on an app powered by NLP and NLU.

Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist on a fast growing start-up company in Vancouver, Istuary Innovation Group. At Istuary Innovation Group, I was mainly developing deep learning algorithms on two products. First is a computer vision related product, the smart camera for Yunnan Department of Public Security e.g., facial recognition, face verification, and face alignment. Second, I was trying to provide NLP features to a Chinese News app in which you input a keyword e.g., internet of thing, the semantic similar news and their automatic summarization will be fetched and presented.

After years of experience working in machine learning related applications, my main interest is in practical applications that can be beneficial to people, such as transfer learning, multi-task learning, and light-weighted models. If you are interested in my experience or my website, have a look at my resume for more information.

 

I write this blog not only to help me refresh my memory about engineering details and theory behind models, training tricks, and optimization but also to communicate with people who might read and share what they know so we can learn from each other.
